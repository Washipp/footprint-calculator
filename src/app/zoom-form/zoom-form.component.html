Zoom-Carbon-Footprint

Input the data according to your average usage. You can calculate this for every lecture or as an average over a week. If you have not used any of these services, leave the number 0.

The input values can overlap, meaning if you have 1 hour of screenshare and listening, you input 1 hour in the first and 1 hour in the second field.

Note that this provides a lower-bound of your usage and some factors aren't taken into consideration. (such as video quality, we always assume its the lowest.)

  • Listening or Talking: This field counts the hours by just being in a Zoom meeting and listening to someone (for example a lecture) or talking.
  • Screenshare: Add how many hours you spent watching someone sharing their screen or you yourself sharing your screen.
  • Webcam usage: The amount of hours you have watched one or several persons using the webcam or you have used the webcam yourself.
